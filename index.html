Computer Keyboard

Controls

  • Play: Click / tap keys, or use keyboard shortcuts above.
  • Octave shift: OCT▼ / OCT▲ buttons (or keyboard [ / ] ).
  • Voice: Select sound in the VOICE panel.
  • Rhythm: Select pattern, press ▶ BEAT to start/stop.
  • TAP: Tap BPM in real time.
  • REC: Record notes. PLAY to replay. LOOP to loop it.
  • Slots: Click SL1–SL4 to SAVE. Shift+click to LOAD.
  • LESSON: Pick a song, pick a mode, press START. Lights guide you.
  • DEMO: Each model has a unique signature demo. Press again to stop.
  • MUSEUM: Background, specs, and catalog quotes for each model.

Lesson Modes

  • Watch: App plays and lights keys. Just listen.
  • Slow: Watch mode at half speed.
  • Follow: Light stays until you play the right key.
  • Practice: Lights guide you, errors don't stop you.
  • Challenge: Scored. Every error counted.
