Controls

  • Play: Click/tap keys or use keyboard shortcuts.
  • Octave: OCT▼/▲ buttons or [ / ] keys.
  • Difficulty: EASY / NORMAL / HARD affects lesson scoring and arcade lives.
  • REC: Record notes (free or quantized). PLAY to replay. LOOP to loop.
  • Q OFF/ON: Toggle quantize on recording.
  • Slots: Click = SAVE · Shift+click = LOAD.
  • LESSON: Pick a song + mode, press START. Follow the lights.
  • ARCADE: Fast-paced scored mode with lives and combos.
  • SONGS: Build your own lesson songs in the step editor.
  • BEATS: Design custom rhythm patterns in the step sequencer.
  • PRESETS: Edit voice parameters and save custom sounds.
  • PERF: Full-screen performance mode.
  • AWARDS: Track achievements unlocked by playing.
